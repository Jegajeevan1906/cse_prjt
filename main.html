About RRASE

RRASE College of Engineering, approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, is governed by the RRASE Health and Educational Trust. Founded by Dr. S. Renganathan, a renowned academician and visionary leader, the Trust reflects his commitment to accessible, high-quality education.


With a legacy that includes roles such as Vice-Chancellor of Bharath University, Dean of MIT, and Director of Research at Anna University, Dr. Renganathan continues to inspire innovation and academic excellence. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure students are prepared to tackle tomorrow's global challenges.
