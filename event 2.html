Artificial Intelligence Immersion

The AI Workshop at RRASE College of Engineering serves as a vital bridge between theoretical classroom concepts and real-world industrial applications. Building on previous seminars, the current workshops focus on high-demand skills for 2026, such as Generative AI, AI Agents, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).


These hands-on sessions in specialized laboratories provide students with experience in building intelligent systems. By collaborating with industry tech firms, the department ensures students master AI Systems Thinking, preparing them to design responsible AI solutions for the modern landscape.
